Welcome to The Monté International School


Monté International is a co-educational & semi residential school, established under the aegis of K.N. Educational Society, as the latest associate of Montessori Group of Schools. The school is spread over a verdant campus in a lush green environs, in the outskirts of Kurnool city, embodying state of art facilities in a specifically designed ambience replete with centralized AC classrooms & Unique sporting facilities. Monté, through modern Learning-Teaching techniques, endeavours to make education a happy, value based and progressive journey that creates the global citizens of tomorrow.



MONTÉ INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Through pursuance of excellence in education, Monté with traditional values of Empathy, Respect and Discipline, renders liberal education where the mind flowers without fear, a vision that accentuates human excellence and moulds the younger generation for a meaningful and productive life, in the globalised society. It aims at the child's active involvement in the process of learning thereby making the educational process child centric and correlated to their life outside the school.
